About Us

At US Reg Support, we are dedicated to guiding biotechnology innovators through the complex regulatory landscape. With deep expertise in U.S. FDA, EMA, and global regulatory frameworks, our mission is to simplify the regulatory process so you can focus on what matters most—bringing life-changing therapies to patients.

Our team of seasoned regulatory affairs professionals has decades of combined experience in cell and gene therapy, biologics, and pharmaceuticals. We partner with biotech companies of all sizes—from early-stage startups to established industry leaders—to develop effective regulatory strategies, author high-quality submission documents, and navigate interactions with health authorities.

What We Do:

  • Strategic regulatory planning from preclinical to post-market phases
  • Preparation and submission of INDs, IMPDs, BLAs, NDAs, and MAAs
  • Regulatory writing and dossier preparation
  • Health authority meeting preparation and representation
  • Compliance audits and gap assessments

US Reg Support prides itself on being more than just consultants—we are collaborators invested in your success. By combining scientific rigor with regulatory insight, we help accelerate development timelines, mitigate risk, and support successful product approvals.
